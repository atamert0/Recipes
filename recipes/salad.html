A flavorful twist to a German classic, this potato salad gets a big boost of flavor from our ham.

Steps

  1. Cook potatoes in boiling salted water until just tender, about 20 minutes.
  2. While potatoes are cooking, heat pan over medium heat and add oil. When oil is hot, add sliced ham and cook until lightly browned with crispy, golden edges, about 5 minutes.
  3. Drain potatoes and allow to cool slightly. While still warm, gently mix potatoes in large bowl with all other ingredients. Taste for seasoning.
